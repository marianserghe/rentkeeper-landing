RentKeeper

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Summary: Your data stays on your device. We never sell or share your information.

1. Introduction

RentKeeper ("we," "our," or "the App") is a rental property management application designed for small landlords. This privacy policy explains how our app handles information when you use it.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Your properties, tenants, payments, expenses, and all other information remain entirely on your iPhone. You can use RentKeeper 100% offline.

  • Your data is stored in your device's local storage
  • Backup files you create are saved to your device's file system
  • We have no access to your data whatsoever

3. Information We Collect

We collect nothing. RentKeeper operates entirely offline. We do not collect personal information, track behavior, or connect to external servers.

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing
  • Store your data on any server

4. Third-Party Services

RentKeeper integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Zillow: When you request a property value estimate, the app queries Zillow's API. This sends the property address to Zillow. zillow.com/privacy

Core features (property management, tenant tracking, payment logging) work 100% offline. Internet is only required for property value estimates.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by industry-standard security measures:

  • Local data: Protected by iOS's built-in encryption when your device is locked
  • Backups: You control where your backup files are stored

We recommend using iOS's built-in backup features (iCloud or local backup) to protect against data loss.

6. Data You Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Create backup files anytime from Settings
  • Delete: Remove individual records or all data via Settings
  • Transfer: Backup files can be moved to other devices

7. Children's Privacy

RentKeeper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we change how the app handles data, we'll update this policy. Significant changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
