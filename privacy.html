RentKeeper

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

TL;DR: Your data stays on your device by default. If you enable cloud sync, your data is stored securely by Supabase. We never sell or share your information.

1. Introduction

RentKeeper ("we," "our," or "the App") is a rental property management application designed for small landlords. This privacy policy explains how our app handles information when you use it.

2. Data Storage

By default, all data is stored locally on your device. Your properties, tenants, payments, expenses, and all other information remain entirely on your iPhone. You can use RentKeeper 100% offline without an account.

  • Your data is stored in your device's local storage
  • Backup files you create are saved to your device's file system
  • We have no access to your local data whatsoever

Optional Cloud Sync: If you choose to create an account and sign in, your data syncs securely to the cloud via Supabase. This enables multi-device access and backup. Your sync data is stored on Supabase's secure infrastructure and is encrypted in transit and at rest.

3. Information We Collect

Without an account: We collect nothing. RentKeeper operates entirely offline. We do not collect personal information, track behavior, or connect to external servers.

With an account (cloud sync): When you sign in, we collect:

  • Email address: Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Your rental data: Properties, tenants, payments, expenses — synced to your account

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing
  • Access your synced data beyond what's necessary to provide the service

4. Third-Party Services

RentKeeper integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Used for optional authentication and cloud sync. Your account credentials and synced data are stored on Supabase's secure infrastructure. supabase.com/privacy
  • Zillow: When you request a property value estimate, the app queries Zillow's API. This sends the property address to Zillow. zillow.com/privacy

Core features (property management, tenant tracking, payment logging) work 100% offline. Internet is only required for cloud sync and property value estimates.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by industry-standard security measures:

  • Local data: Protected by iOS's built-in encryption when your device is locked
  • Cloud data: Encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest by Supabase
  • Authentication: Handled securely by Supabase Auth

We recommend using iOS's built-in backup features (iCloud or local backup) to protect against data loss when using the app offline.

6. Data You Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Create backup files anytime from Settings
  • Delete: Remove individual records or all data via Settings
  • Transfer: Backup files can be moved to other devices
  • Account deletion: Contact us to delete your account and all associated data

7. Children's Privacy

RentKeeper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we change how the app handles data, we'll update this policy. Significant changes will be noted in the app's release notes and may require your consent.
