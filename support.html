Getting Started

RentKeeper is designed to be simple and intuitive. Here's how to get started:

  • Add a Property: Tap the "+" button on the Properties tab
  • Add Units: For multi-family properties, add units within each property
  • Track Payments: Go to the Payments tab and tap "+" to record rent payments
  • Log Expenses: Use the Expenses tab to track costs, set up recurring expenses, and attach receipt photos
  • Backup Data: Go to Settings → Backup & Export to save your data locally

Common Questions

Q: Is my data stored in the cloud?

A: Your data is stored locally on your device. You can export backups anytime from Settings.

Q: Can I use RentKeeper offline?

A: Yes! The app works 100% offline. Internet is only needed for property value estimates via Zillow.

Q: How do I backup my data?

A: Go to Settings → Backup & Export to create manual backups. Auto-backup keeps your data safe locally.

Q: How do I get property value estimates?

A: Tap on a property and look for the Zestimate section. This queries Zillow's API for current estimates.

Q: Is my data private?

A: Yes. We never sell or share your information. Read our full Privacy Policy.

Troubleshooting

App won't open?

  • Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Try restarting your device
  • Check if you have enough storage space

Can't sign in?

RentKeeper works without an account. Simply start adding properties!
